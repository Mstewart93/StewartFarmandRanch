About

Here at Stewart Farm and Ranch, we belive that it is critical to know where food comes from.

Based on this idea, we carefully, raise all of our animals with the utmost of care. Every animal is cared for from their beginning all the way to their end. From first breath forward, our animals are part of the family. Now, we do raise our livestock for meat as well. The way that we at Stewart Farm and Ranch look at it is, we are going to eat meat for a variety of reasons, I would rather be confident in the life the animal lived, than wonder the conditions it lived in prior to processing. That leads us to our promise to you.

Our Promise to You

At Stewart Farm and Ranch, our livestock are always humanely raised and cared for. The KuneKune pigs graze grass in the summer, you can learn more about that here, and are given a grain/alfalfa feed when grass is infrequent. Likewise, our chickens are alowed to forage naturally all summer long, they also are suplimented with graina and Alfalfa hay in the winter.
Because of this you can feel confident in the quality of our products!
